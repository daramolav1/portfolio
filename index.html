About Victor

I came across Thinkful after reading reviews on several other bootcamps. I went with Thinkful because I've seen nothing but positive reviews and I'm looking forward to learning as much as I can and finally being able to break into the tech industry as a Web Developer/Software Engineer

I'm very passionate about web development because I love how content from other developers' imagination came to life. Things millions of people use everyday like Netflix, Instagram, and Uber gives users convenience and entertainment at the same time. I want the opportunity as well to make the lives of others easier and have my imagination come to life.

Working well with others includes being willing to take responsibility for things that go wrong and praise for things that go right, and never trying to hog the glory. Also, respecting the contributions of everyone on your team, and welcome collaboration. Teamwork goes a long way and that is a necessity in becoming a successful developer.

In my free time other than coding, I like to workout or play video games on the PlayStation 5.
